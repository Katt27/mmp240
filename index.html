ABOUT ME

Hello! I have so many nicknames but two that sticks are Kathy or Cheeky (my chubby cheeks).

and I are stable and okay. In order to honor that and his love for education, I came back to BMCC
to obtain my Associates in Science with the hopes of transfering to NYU and obtaining a Bachelor's
in Fine Arts.
